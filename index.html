Welcome to the CrowdSmart Commons

Collective reasoing creates live knowledge models from social conversations and discussions. This is not a survey but a social engagement where we reason together about a topic. What is different about this experience is that we collectively create a predictive knowledge model from our discussion. As a participant you will see other's reasoning and thinking about a topic. Do you agree with them? You will get a chance to indicate your alignement with their thinking? Did an idea you viewed spark a new idea? Share your view and see if others align with you. The system is driven by an adaptive learning algorithm that creates a collective knowledge model of our collective view of a prediction or decision.
